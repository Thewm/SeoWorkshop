" Real world Example "

Imagine you have a shop that selling mobile phones, back covers and charges. The title of your homepage should include all 3 keywords like;
My shop - New Phones, Trendy covers and fast charging chargers
Now you should have at least 3 pages on your site, one dedicated to phones and the other for covers and one for chargers
Title for the homepage should be a title for your entire site. The title for the sub pages in your site should be related to the sub category and not universal for the entire site .
In wordpress you can set it up for each page on your site and for HTML it goes between the title tags .
